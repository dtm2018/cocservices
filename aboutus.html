About-Us


Circle of Care's goal is to attain and possibly exceed perfection in the delivery of person centered healthcare services to people with intellectual disabilities. We believe that the satisfaction of people placed in our agency is our utmost responsibility. As a result, ensuring their happiness is our duty and responsibility and we are fully committed to it.

We work closely as a team to ensure that the people we work for achieve their goals in life. We are happy and relent only when the people we serve satisfactorily achieve their goals and succeed in living decent high quality independent lives in their communities. Our staff, board members, volunteers, and communities work together to address the needs of each of the individuals we support. In doing so, we encourage and develop each person’s dream of self-direction and individual participation in their communities, using the skills and knowledge gained through participation in our programs. Circle of Care is a provider of choice with a top notch Quality Assurance department that promptly follows through every incident to ensure the welfare of the individuals.

Circle of Care is an equal opportunity employer. Our employees receive rigorous background checks and are selected based on previous experience, excellent recommendations, and their passion for serving others. We treat both persons and employees like family.

In accordance with DDS regulations, Circle of Care ensures that all employees:   

  • Have clean criminal backgrounds,
  • Are completely free from communicable diseases,
  • Complete phases one through four of the DDS mandated trainings,
  • Are authorized to work in the United States,
  • Can communicate (read, write, speak and understand) English language. Some of our employees are bilingual.
