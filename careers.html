Careers


Circle of Care is an equal opportunity employer. Our employees receive rigorous background checks and are selected based on previous experience, excellent recommendations, and their passion for serving others. We treat both persons and employees like family. Circle of Care employs people who are inspired by the challenge of meeting the daily needs of people of differing abilities. We provide extensive training and support to all staff in order to ensure the highest quality outcomes for the individuals we serve.

We are currently looking for skilled professionals to join our winning team of healthcare professionals. The following positions are open:

  • Residential Counselors (DSP - Direct Support Professionals)
  • QIDP (Qualified Intellectual Disability Professional)
  • DON (Director of Nursing)
  • PD (Program Director)
  • APD (Assistant Program Director).
